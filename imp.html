Recommended Settings

  • Use Balanced/HD graphics for smooth gameplay.
  • Always keep FPS high for better recoil control.
  • Gyroscope ON helps a lot in close fights.
  • Customize controls according to comfort.

Safety Tips

  • Never download hacks — they will ban your account.
  • Enable secure login and protect your BGMI ID.
  • Avoid logging in on untrusted phones.

Match Preparation

  • Keep device temperature low to avoid lag.
  • Use stable internet connection.
  • Communicate with your squad before dropping.
